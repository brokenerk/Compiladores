Which functionalities offer and compilers topics integrate?
  • Creation, operations and visualization of Non deterministic Finite Automatas
  • Conversion from Non deterministic Finite Automatas to Deterministic Finite Automatas and visualization of its tabular form
  • Lexical Analysis of Deterministic Finite Automatas through tokens
  • Creation of Deterministic Finite Automatas and Grammars using recursive descent analysis
  • Grammars and strings parsers using LL(1), LR(0), LR(1) and LALR analysis
